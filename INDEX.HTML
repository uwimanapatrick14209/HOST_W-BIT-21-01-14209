Why Study With Us?

01
Sustained provision of Industry-relevant & Globally-recognized Academic and Professional Programmes

At UoK, you can choose from our wide range of industry-relevant and globally recognised academic and professional programmes. These include undergraduate, graduate, professional, language, and executive programmes in fields such as Business Information Technology, Marketing, Accounting, Finance, Economics, Law, Procurement & Supply Chain Management, Computer Science, Information Technology, Business Administration, Early Childhood Development Education, CPA (Rwanda), CPA (Kenya), ACCA, CIPS, CIA, IPSAS, and more.

02
Multiple Campuses and Easily-accessible Locations

University of Kigali has 2 campuses situated at vantage locations in Rwanda. The university’s main campus is located in Kigali, next to the plush Kigali Heights and about 3 kilometers from the Kigali City Centre. The second campus is located within the RSSB Building in Musanze, in the northern district. These campuses and locations make it easy for local as well as international students to access quality higher education at UoK.

03
Six World-class & Well-equipped Schools

UoK has six world-class and well-equipped schools which use effective and globally-acclaimed resources to deliver excellent academic services to students in diverse fields of study. The schools are:

  • Graduate School
  • School of Business Management & Economics
  • School of Computing & Information Technology
  • School of Education
  • School of Law
  • School of Professional and Executive Programmes.
04
Experienced and Diverse Faculty

UoK has diverse and experienced faculty from Rwanda, India, Kenya, Uganda, Nigeria, United Kingdom and United States of America. Currently, our programme modules are taught by 9 Professors and 32 PhD-holders in various disciplines, with exposure from different parts of the world and vast experience in both industry practice and academia.

05
International Partnerships

UoK believes in diversity, creation and dissemination of critical knowledge embedded in the wealth and potential of the region and the world. Consequently, we have partnered with reputable universities and institutions in Kenya, Uganda, India, South Africa, Germany, Italy, Canada, UK, Poland and Ghana among others to give students and staff international exchange opportunities. Some of our exchange programmes are AIESEC, Erasmus and Erasmus+ through the support of the European Union.

06
Massive International Student Population

As a university of choice for international students with a passionate drive towards internationalization and excellence, UoK has an international office which is solely responsible for the welfare of international students from the time of entry till they graduate. Currently, we have students from Angola, Nigeria, Gabon, Ivory Coast, Ethiopia, Sierra Leone, Malawi, China, Turkey, Burundi, Uganda, Kenya, DR Congo, India, Chad, etc.

07
Flexible Payment Plan & Platforms

University of Kigali has a flexible plan for tuition fee payment which allows students to pay fees either on monthly basis, per trimester or annually. There are also modern and flexible payment modes such as local and foreign bank transfers as well as mobile platforms.
